## Project Overview

**Application Name:** Fruit Crops Investment

**Concept:** A digital investment platform that operates similarly to a stock exchange, where users invest in individual fruit trees rather than traditional stocks or shares. Returns are generated based on actual agricultural harvest cycles.

---

## Core Application Requirements

### 1. User Management System

User Registration & Authentication

- Secure sign-up/login functionality (email, phone, social media OAuth)
- KYC (Know Your Customer) verification process
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- User profile management with investment history

User Roles

- **Investors:** Individual users who purchase fruit tree investments
- **Farm Owners/Partners:** Agricultural partners who list their farms and crops
- **Administrators:** Platform managers overseeing operations

---

### 2. Investment Marketplace

Farm Listings

- Comprehensive farm profiles including:
  - Farm name, location (with map integration)
  - Farm size and total capacity
  - Certification status (organic, sustainable, etc.)
  - Historical performance data
  - Farm owner/operator credentials
  - Climate and soil conditions
  - High-resolution images and virtual tours

Fruit Crop Variants

Create detailed listings for each investable fruit tree type, including but not limited to:

| **Fruit Type** | **Variants to Include** |
| --- | --- |
| Durian | Musang King, D24, Black Thorn, Red Prawn |
| Mango | Alphonso, Nam Doc Mai, Carabao, Kent |
| Grapes | Thompson Seedless, Concord, Shine Muscat |
| Melon | Honeydew, Cantaloupe, Yubari King |
| Citrus | Valencia Orange, Meyer Lemon, Pomelo |
| Others | Avocado, Longan, Rambutan, Mangosteen |

Investment Details Per Tree

- **Price per tree** (initial investment cost)
- **Expected ROI percentage**
- **Harvest cycle/frequency** (annual, bi-annual, seasonal)
- **Tree age and expected productive lifespan**
- **Historical yield data**
- **Risk assessment rating**
- **Minimum/maximum investment limits**

---

### 3. Investment Tracking Dashboard

Real-Time Portfolio Management

- Total investment value
- Number of trees owned (by farm and crop type)
- Current growth stage indicators
- Expected harvest dates (calendar view)
- Projected returns vs. actual returns
- Portfolio diversification visualization

Tree Health Monitoring

- Live updates on crop conditions
- Weather impact alerts
- Pest/disease notifications
- Growth progress photos/updates from farms

---

### 4. Harvest & Returns System

Harvest Schedule Management

- Automated harvest date notifications
- Yield estimation updates
- Market price tracking for each fruit type
- Harvest completion confirmations

Profit Distribution

- Transparent calculation methodology
- Multiple payout options:
  - Direct bank transfer
  - Digital wallet
  - Reinvestment option
- Detailed transaction history
- Tax documentation/reports

---

### 5. Financial Features

Transaction Capabilities

- Secure payment gateway integration
- Multiple currency support
- Investment top-up functionality
- Secondary market (optional): Allow users to sell their tree investments to other users

Financial Reporting

- Profit/loss statements
- Investment performance analytics
- Downloadable reports (PDF, CSV)
- Year-end tax summaries

---

### 6. Information & Education Center

Investment Education

- How fruit tree investing works
- Risk factors and mitigation
- Understanding harvest cycles
- Market trend analysis

Farm & Crop Encyclopedia

- Detailed information about each fruit type
- Growing conditions and requirements
- Market demand insights
- Seasonality charts

---

### 7. Communication Features

- In-app messaging system
- Push notifications for:
  - Investment opportunities
  - Harvest updates
  - Payment confirmations
  - Market alerts
- Email notifications
- Customer support chat
